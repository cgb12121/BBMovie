Go here to download whisper-cpp model: https://huggingface.co/ggerganov/whisper.cpp/tree/main
Select: ggml-base.en.bin or ggml-base.en-q5_1.bin (cheaper)
Put the model file in ../models/whisper-cpp/

Then put the model name in rust-ai-context-refinery/src/services/whisper.rs
